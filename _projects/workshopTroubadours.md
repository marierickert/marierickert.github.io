In popularizing publications, I like to make research processes visible, not only focus on results. Examples are [this blog on taalwijs.nu](https://taalwijs.nu/2024/05/21/taaldiversiteit-op-de-kinderopvang/) and my chapter in Elsje Fourie’s and Christine Hoene’s [book](https://library.maastrichtuniversity.nl/resources/maastricht-university-press/catalog/the-stories-we-tell/) ‘The stories we tell’ (available in Dutch and English versions, Maastricht University Press & OA).
